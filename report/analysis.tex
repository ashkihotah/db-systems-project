\section{Requirements Analysis}

The concepts can be reorganized as follows:


\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{|p{15cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Central Office} \\
\hline
The central office does not handle physical setups \\
\hline
Both the central office and depots are characterized by name, address, city/province, and number of employees \\
\hline
Bookings, either recurring or one-time, are coordinated by the central office \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{table}


\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{|p{15cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Depot} \\
\hline
Several operational depots located across the world \\
\hline
Each depot covers a specific region \\
\hline
Both the central office and depots are characterized by name, address, city/province, and number of employees \\
\hline
Depots have one or more setup teams, each with a maximum number of members \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{table}


\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{|p{15cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Region} \\
\hline
A specific region covered by each depot \\
\hline
Each region consists of one or more municipalities \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{table}


\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{|p{15cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Booking} \\
\hline
Bookings can be either recurring or one-time \\
\hline
Bookings are coordinated by the central office \\
\hline
Bookings can be placed by customers via phone, postal mail, email, or through the website \\
\hline
The company handles seasonal contracts and special promotional rentals \\
\hline
Bookings are delivered and installed at customer event locations \\
\hline
Each booking is characterized by type, date, duration, cost, the team that handled it, and the details of the customer who placed it \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{table}


\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{|p{15cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Customer} \\
\hline
Customers are classified as individuals or companies \\
\hline
Customers are identified by an alphanumeric code and their personal details \\
\hline
Customers can place bookings via phone, postal mail, email, or through the website \\
\hline
Each customer can have one or more event locations \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{table}


\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{|p{15cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Event Location} \\
\hline
Bookings are delivered and installed at customer event locations \\
\hline
Event locations are characterized by address, house number, postal code, city, and province \\
\hline
Each address is identified by a unique code within the customer's profile \\
\hline
Each event location has a setup time estimate and equipment capacity \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{table}


\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{|p{15cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Setup Team} \\
\hline
Depots have one or more setup teams \\
\hline
Each team has a maximum number of members \\
\hline
Each team is characterized by an identification code and a name \\
\hline
Each team is tracked by the number of installations made \\
\hline
Team members have personal data stored \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{table}


\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{|p{15cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Equipment} \\
\hline
Audiovisual equipment is managed for the rental and installation for events \\
\hline
Each event location has equipment capacity \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

\subsection{Glossary of Terms}

\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{|p{2.2cm}|p{4cm}|p{3.5cm}|p{3.5cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Concept} & \textbf{Description} & \textbf{Connections} & \textbf{Synonyms} \\
\hline
Central Office & The main coordination center that does not handle physical setups but coordinates all bookings & Depot, Booking & - \\
\hline
Depot & Operational centers located across the world, characterized by name, address, city/province, and number of employees & Region, Setup Team & Operational Depot \\
\hline
Region & Geographic areas covered by depots, consisting of one or more municipalities & Depot, Municipality & - \\
\hline
Booking & Service requests that can be recurring or one-time, placed via various channels and characterized by type, date, duration, cost, team, and customer details & Customer, Event Location, Setup Team & - \\
\hline
Customer & Individuals or companies identified by alphanumeric code and personal details who place bookings and have event locations & Booking, Event Location & - \\
\hline
Event Location & Specific places characterized by address, house number, postal code, city, and province where bookings are delivered and installed & Customer, Booking & - \\
\hline
Setup Team & Groups of installation personnel working at depots, identified by code and name, tracked by number of installations and member personal data & Depot, Members & Setup Team, Installation Team, Team \\
\hline
Installations & Audiovisual devices managed for rental and installation at events & Booking & Audiovisual Equipment, Equipment \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{table}
